About

Hello there! I'm Tomás Tapia, an IT Solution Architect with over fifteen years of enriching experience in technical leadership and full-stack development. My professional journey is a testament to a proactive approach and a deep customer-centric philosophy. Agile methodologies have reshaped my project strategies, making my workflow streamlined and adaptive. As I navigate through the cloud computing expanse, I'm honing my skills in AWS, GCP, and Azure, pursuing a CKAD certification, and delving into multi-cloud strategies. The cloud-native landscape fascinates me, especially the transformative potential of serverless architectures.

On the technical front, my expertise spans across versatile programming languages and frameworks. Fluent in Golang, Java complemented by SpringBoot, Node.js, and TypeScript, I also craft engaging user interfaces with the finesse of Vue.js and Angular. My role as a Cloud Solutions Architect has empowered me to design scalable cloud infrastructures that underscore my commitment to creating robust, efficient solutions. As a lifelong learner of the digital domain, I'm always eager to collaborate and push the boundaries of technology to new horizons. Let's connect and drive innovation together!
