About

Hello! I’m Tomás Tapia, an IT Solution Architect with over fifteen years of experience in technical leadership and software development. My career has been built on a hands-on approach, a strong customer focus, and the ability to guide teams through complex projects. I work with cloud platforms like AWS, GCP, and Azure, helping companies design and run systems that are scalable, secure, and cost-efficient. I’m especially interested in cloud-native architectures, including serverless and container-based solutions, because of their potential to transform how businesses deliver value.

On the development side, I have experience with Golang, Java (Spring Boot), Node.js, and TypeScript, as well as building modern interfaces with Vue.js and Angular. My goal is always the same: create reliable, efficient solutions that make a real impact on both users and the business. I also enjoy mentoring engineers and leading teams, encouraging best practices and helping people grow in their careers. Technology is always moving, and I see myself as a lifelong learner, eager to keep improving and sharing knowledge.

If you’re working on something exciting and want to talk about cloud, architecture, or innovation, let’s connect.
